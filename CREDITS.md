# Credits

## KittyScan

The idea for this blocklist - publishing the scanner IPs detected by our Minecraft
honeypots as a public, regularly-updated blocklist for server
operators to use - was inspired by **KittyScan** and its `KittyScanBlocklist`.

Thanks to the KittyScan project for this approach. MCArchiveBlocklist is an
independent effort with its own honeypots, detections, and tooling, but it follows the
same spirit: transparency about scanning activity and giving operators an easy way to
defend their servers.

KittyScan: https://kittyscan.com, https://github.com/LillySchramm, https://github.com/LillySchramm/KittyScanBlocklist
